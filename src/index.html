ATC Buddy
v1.2.0 (Latest)

ATC Buddy is a desktop application in development and contains a small suite of ATC tools for use on the VATSIM network. The goal of this project is to increase the simplicity of controlling, lower the amount of human mistakes caused by complex 'Letters of Agreements' and 'Standard Operating Procedures', and promote a generally more pleasureable ATC experience.

Credits:
